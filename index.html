About Me

My journey in tech started with a curiosity to understand how the web pages and software we use every day work. I decided to use online resources to learn more and became hooked. Now, I spend my time doing programming projects in Python, Java, Django, HTML and CSS to keep building on my knowledge.

The blog linked to this page is another way for me to express the lessons I have learnt while gaining new programming skills and features tech and life related topics. There is a lot to learn and the journey has been exciting and filled with a lot of reward.

In my spare time I enjoy keeping fit, watching series, having conversations with people and reading.
